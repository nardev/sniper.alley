The Sniper Alley Photo Archive is a non profit effort to find, identify, and preserve photographs taken in Sarajevo during the siege, 1992 to 1996.

Your support helps us digitize archives, keep the collection online, and produce new Stories Behind Photo.

Donation options are being prepared. Until they are ready, please write to [info@sniperalley.photo](mailto:info@sniperalley.photo) if you would like to support the project.

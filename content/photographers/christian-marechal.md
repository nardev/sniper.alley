    caption: "The lethal Skenderija junction. The stack of shipping containers on the left are a partial shield against hillside snipers who pick off people hurrying across the interesection. The broad sweep of Marshall Tito Street on the left becomes Sniper Alley -- the airport road. To avoid it, and to reach the Holiday Inn, you fork right, where the white car emerges. (Most cars taking that fork go straight across the tongue of sidewalk that sticks out from the corner on the right to the street light in the centre of the shot.) The turn on the far right is Kralja Tomislava, the street where President Izetbegovic -- and the Musakadic family -- have apartments. It's also the road to Tuzla, home base to UNPROFOR's British contingent. Tomislava Street climbs uphill after the first few yards, offering a clear line of fire over the containers."

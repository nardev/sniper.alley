Dominik Landwehr (*1958) has been working most of his life in the field of journalismu and culture, during 4 years he was also a delegate of the International Comittee of the Red Cross ICRC. The photos presented here were shot in 1995 in Sarajevo shortly before the peace accord of Daytonwhen he was doing research for an article.
